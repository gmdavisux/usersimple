Replenishing Suppliers

The legacy landing page for suppliers offered very little actionable content. Suppliers immediately left this page looking for actual work to be done.

User Research

Our research found that suppliers were looking for actual work to be done. They were not interested in reading about the company or its values. They wanted to get started on their tasks as soon as possible.
