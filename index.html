Gary M Davis

An experienced designer improving the world through human-centered problem-solving and design.

Over 25 years crafting intuitive experinces

After a background in industrial design and graphic design, I spent the last 20 years focusing exclusively on designing elegant and intuitive user expereinces for business productivity tools. I use design thinking processes and tools to understand users, reframe problems, design, prototype and test.

View my work

Productivity

Most of my work has specialized in designing interfaces for business-to-business productivity tools that improve workflows and efficiency.

Usability

I use interviews, testing, and observation to truly understand people and their problems. Hands-on research guides me in creating intuitive interfaces that just make sense for the end-user to provide a delightful, natural experience.

Simplicity

Simplicity, without innovation to enable it, may make a design look nice but perform poorly. I focus on innovation to enable simplicity.

Design

With a keen eye for design and user needs, I craft flowing, distraction-free interfaces for seamless user experiences.
