Innovation

Innovation unlocks new possibilities through technology. I actively explore ways to leverage innovative tech solutions that can eliminate unnecessary work or transform existing processes. Rather than simply meeting stated requirements, I strive to reframe the problem itself to uncover more optimal paths forward.

Usability

I use interviews, testing, and observation to truly understand people and their problems. Hands-on research guides me in creating intuitive interfaces that just make sense for the end-user to provide a delightful, natural experience.

Simplicity

Simplicity, without innovation to enable it, may make a design look nice but perform poorly. I focus on innovation to enable simplicity.

Design

Good design delights users through intuitive and appealing experiences. I leverage design principles to effectively communicate, convey meaning, and encourage desired behaviors.
